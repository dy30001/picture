## 1. Global Rules

- Bride wears no glasses in all wedding outfit anchors and final wedding images.
- Face must stay clear: no veil, hair, jewelry, fan, bouquet, sleeve, or pose covering key facial features.
- Styling may change by scene; face, age impression, body scale, and height relationship must not change.
- Avoid cheap stagewear, cosplay, plastic fabric, heavy beauty filter, and influencer-template makeup.
- Groom styling coordinates with the bride but stays refined, simple, and believable.

## 2. Style Families

| Code | Style Family | Bride Styling | Groom Styling | Best Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| A | Main White Gown | White or ivory main gown, veil behind face, refined train | Black tuxedo or formal groom suit | Landmark cover, ceremony mood, wide and medium shots |
| B | Light Destination | Satin/chiffon light dress, easy movement, soft white or ivory | Beige, light gray, or soft neutral suit | Walking, travel, documentary, relaxed daylight shots |
| C | Evening Editorial | Black, burgundy, gold, or deep charcoal evening gown | Black tuxedo, velvet, or dark formal suit | Night scenes, architecture, low-angle editorial shots |
| D | French Couture | French lace, corset or square neckline, pearl/veil detail, vintage mood | Black tuxedo, bow tie, refined boutonniere | Paris, Prague, Venice, cafe/street/architecture scenes |
| E | Modern Chinese | Red or ivory-red modern Chinese bridal set, refined embroidery | Dark formal suit or modern Chinese formal jacket | Kyoto, Chinese-style close-ups, ceremonial frames |
| F | Qipao | Modern bridal qipao, silk/satin texture, high collar, refined slit | Dark suit or mandarin-collar formal jacket | Portraits, evening streets, architectural lines |
| G | Mermaid Gown | Elegant fishtail/mermaid gown, sculpted waist, restrained train | Black or ivory formal suit | Full-body figure shots, city/editorial scenes |
| H | Grand Train | Cathedral-length or grand trailing gown, dramatic but refined volume | Formal black tuxedo or classic groom suit | Ultra-wide landmark covers, stairs, palace, tower, cathedral scenes |

## 4. Prompt Additions

### G Mermaid Gown

```text
Elegant fishtail mermaid bridal gown, sculpted waist, refined satin or lace texture, tasteful body line, restrained train, sophisticated and bridal, not over-sexualized, no cheap stagewear.
```

### H Grand Train

```text
Grand cathedral-length trailing wedding gown, dramatic refined train, luxurious fabric volume, elegant ceremonial silhouette, full body and entire train visible, not costume-like, not overdone.
```
