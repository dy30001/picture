# 婚纱旅拍提示词系统

版本：2026-04-26  
模式：`doc mode`  
适用范围：人物一致性、三视图、81 张成片单张提示词、负面提示词、返修提示词。  
依据文件：`planning/03-流程与提示词/04-婚纱生成工作流.md`、`planning/07-结构化清单/81张镜头清单.csv`。

## 0. 使用原则

- 每次只生成一张图，不用一个笼统提示词批量生成多张。
- 人物身份提示词必须置于场景、服装、构图之前。
- 所有成片都必须锁定同一对新人，不允许换脸、换年龄感、换肤色、换身高关系。
- 地标必须真实可识别，但不能像抠图贴背景。
- 风格以真实小红书旅拍、电影感、自然情绪、高级婚纱质感为主。
- 三视图只用于人物基准，不计入 81 张成片。

## 1. 人物一致性总提示词模板

该模板作为所有三视图、样片和 81 张成片的固定前缀。执行时用 `character_bible.md` 和已筛选参考图补齐字段。

```text
Use case: identity-preserve / photorealistic-natural
Asset type: 4K luxury destination wedding photography
Primary request:
Generate one photorealistic image of the same bride and groom from the selected reference images.

Global identity lock:
- Preserve the bride as the exact same person from the reference images.
- Bride identity details: {bride_face_shape}, {bride_eyes}, {bride_nose}, {bride_lips}, {bride_skin_tone}, {bride_hairstyle}, {bride_body_proportion}, {bride_temperament}.
- Preserve the groom as the exact same person from the reference images.
- Groom identity details: {groom_face_shape}, {groom_eyes}, {groom_nose}, {groom_lips}, {groom_skin_tone}, {groom_hairstyle}, {groom_body_proportion}, {groom_temperament}.
- Preserve the couple's height relationship: {couple_height_relationship}.
- Preserve the couple's intimacy style: {couple_interaction_style}.
- Keep natural facial expressions, realistic skin texture, and believable body proportions.
- Do not change either person into a different ethnicity, celebrity-like face, model-template face, younger face, older face, or generic AI face.

Relationship lock:
- They should look like a real couple with relaxed trust, not strangers posing together.
- Their body distance, eye contact, hand contact, and posture should match the interaction in the reference images.
- Keep intimacy elegant, restrained, and suitable for high-end wedding photography.
```

### 1.1 人物字段来源

| 字段 | 来源 | 填写要求 |
|---|---|---|
| `{bride_*}` | `character_bible.md` 女主身份锁定 | 用短语描述，不写模糊词 |
| `{groom_*}` | `character_bible.md` 男主身份锁定 | 用短语描述，不写模糊词 |
| `{couple_height_relationship}` | `character_bible.md` 双人关系锁定 | 明确身高差和站位关系 |
| `{couple_interaction_style}` | `character_bible.md` 双人关系锁定 | 明确自然互动、亲密尺度 |

## 2. 三视图提示词模板

三视图目标是建立人物身份和比例基准，不追求婚纱大片效果。背景、服装、光线都要中性，避免把场景风格带入人物定型。

### 2.1 女主三视图

```text
{人物一致性总提示词模板}

Turnaround request:
Generate a neutral full-body character reference image of the bride only.

View:
- Required view: {front_view | left_side_view | back_view}
- Full body visible from head to toe.
- Standing naturally, arms relaxed, posture upright.

Wardrobe:
- Simple semi-formal fitted outfit, neutral color.
- No wedding dress, no veil, no dramatic accessories.
- Keep hairstyle consistent with the reference images.

Studio setup:
- Plain light gray or warm off-white background.
- Soft even studio lighting.
- 85mm portrait lens feel.
- No heavy makeup, no beauty filter, no fashion editorial pose.

Quality:
- Realistic face and body.
- Stable hairstyle and body proportion.
- Clean full-body reference suitable for later identity matching.
```

## 3. 81 张成片单张提示词组装规则

每张成片由固定模块按顺序组装。CSV 每一行对应一张图，必须独立生成提示词。

### 3.1 组装顺序

```text
1. 人物一致性总提示词模板
2. 单张主请求
3. CSV 场景字段
4. CSV 服装字段
5. CSV 构图字段
6. 摄影和画质字段
7. 风格字段
8. 通用负面提示词
9. 按需追加返修提示词
```

### 3.2 CSV 字段映射

| CSV 字段 | 提示词字段 | 用法 |
|---|---|---|
| `id` | `{image_id}` | 文件名、元数据、追踪返修 |
| `scene_number` | `{scene_number}` | 场景编号 |
| `scene_slug` | `{scene_slug}` | 输出目录和文件名 |
| `scene_name` | `{scene_name}` | 地点名称 |
| `landmark` | `{landmark}` | 主要地标 |
| `outfit_code` | `{outfit_code}` | A/B/C 服装分类 |
| `outfit_name` | `{outfit_name}` | 该图服装名称 |
| `shot_number` | `{shot_number}` | 每景区 01 到 09 |
| `shot_type` | `{shot_type}` | 景别和镜头功能 |
| `orientation` | `{orientation}` | `vertical` 或 `horizontal` |
| `composition` | `{composition}` | 构图约束 |
| `pose_interaction` | `{pose_interaction}` | 动作和互动 |
| `light` | `{light}` | 时间与光线 |
| `style_goal` | `{style_goal}` | 单张审美目标 |
| `output_folder` | `{output_folder}` | 输出目录 |

### 3.3 单张主模板

```text
{人物一致性总提示词模板}

Single image request:
Generate image {image_id}, one photorealistic 4K destination wedding photograph of the same bride and groom.

Scene:
- Location: {scene_name}
- Landmark: {landmark}
- The landmark must be recognizable, geographically believable, and naturally integrated into the image.
- Do not distort the landmark or make it look like a fake backdrop.

Wardrobe:
- Outfit code: {outfit_code}
- Outfit name: {outfit_name}
- Bride wardrobe: high-end wedding styling matching {outfit_name}, refined fabric, believable tailoring, elegant movement.
- Groom wardrobe: coordinated high-end suit or outfit matching the bride and the location.
- Wardrobe must look luxurious, realistic, and not theatrical.

Composition:
- Orientation: {orientation}
- Shot type: {shot_type}
- Framing and layout: {composition}
- Pose and interaction: {pose_interaction}
- Keep the couple naturally connected to the environment.
- Avoid stiff studio-template posing.

Light and mood:
- Time and light: {light}
- Style goal: {style_goal}
- Natural cinematic light, realistic shadows, elegant color grading.
- Xiaohongshu-inspired destination wedding photography with true emotion and relaxed travel atmosphere.

Camera and quality:
- Photorealistic luxury wedding photography.
- 4K final quality, ultra high resolution.
- Realistic skin texture, natural hands, believable fabric texture.
- Clean background, no watermark, no text, no logo.
```

### 3.4 服装 A/B/C 补充规则

| 服装 | 用途 | 提示词补充 |
|---|---|---|
| A 主纱 | 封面、地标、仪式感 | `main wedding gown, refined veil, high-end bridal tailoring, ceremonial but natural` |
| B 轻旅拍 | 行走、抓拍、互动 | `light destination wedding styling, relaxed movement, travel editorial feeling, natural documentary moment` |
| C 地域融合礼服 | 夜景、建筑透视、细节 | `regional color inspiration, high-end evening gown feeling, cultural hint without costume, cinematic editorial mood` |

### 3.5 镜头编号补充规则

| 镜头 | 必须强化 | 避免 |
|---|---|---|
| 01 | 地标完整、封面感、双人完整入镜 | 人物过小到看不清身份 |
| 02 | 地标中远景、主纱仪式感 | 地标被裁断或贴图感 |
| 03 | 半身亲密、表情、头纱或眼神 | 五官漂移、过度亲吻 |
| 04 | 全身动态、真实抓拍、行走感 | 动作僵硬、裙摆畸形 |
| 05 | 中景纪实、回眸或整理动作 | 表演感过强 |
| 06 | 背影或侧背影、故事感 | 完全看不出双人关系 |
| 07 | 建筑透视、地域礼服、空间高级感 | 人物被建筑压扁 |
| 08 | 服装细节、手部戒指、材质 | 手部畸形、广告硬拍 |
| 09 | 夜景或黄金时刻、收尾封面 | 过暗、剪影无层次 |

## 4. 负面提示词

负面提示词分为通用必带和按需追加。通用必带用于所有图片。

### 4.1 通用必带负面提示词

```text
Do not create a different couple.
Do not change facial identity, ethnicity, age impression, skin tone, hairstyle, body proportion, or height relationship.
Do not create celebrity-like faces, generic model faces, plastic faces, or over-beautified AI faces.
Do not add extra people, extra bride, extra groom, duplicated faces, duplicated bodies, or background strangers as main subjects.
Do not create distorted hands, extra fingers, missing fingers, broken arms, deformed shoulders, deformed necks, crossed eyes, melted faces, asymmetrical eyes, or unnatural teeth.
Do not use plastic skin, heavy beauty filter, oversaturated HDR, fake bokeh, low-resolution texture, posterized color, watermark, logo, text, frame, or UI overlay.
Do not make the wedding dress look cheap, theatrical, costume-like, shiny plastic, badly fitted, or stage-performance style.
Do not distort the landmark, change the landmark into a fantasy structure, or paste the couple onto a fake background.
Do not create mismatched lighting between people and environment.
Do not use stiff studio poses, exaggerated smiles, finger hearts, tourist snapshot style, or repetitive influencer check-in poses.
Do not crop off important body parts unless the shot type is explicitly close-up.
```

## 5. 返修提示词分类

返修时保留原始 CSV 字段和人物一致性总提示词，只追加对应问题的修正模块。每张最多返修 3 轮；3 轮仍不达标时，重写该图单张提示词。

### 5.1 人脸不像

```text
Repair focus: facial identity correction.
Keep the same composition, outfit, pose, lighting, and location.
Only improve identity matching.
Make the bride's face much closer to the selected bride references.
Make the groom's face much closer to the selected groom references.
Preserve age impression, skin tone, hairstyle, facial proportions, and natural expression.
Do not beautify into generic model faces.
```

### 5.2 身高关系或体态错误

```text
Repair focus: couple proportion correction.
Keep the same scene, outfit, pose, and light.
Correct the couple's height relationship to match the reference images.
Make body proportions realistic and stable.
Avoid elongated legs, tiny heads, oversized shoulders, stiff necks, or mismatched scale between the two people.
```

### 5.3 手部和肢体畸形

```text
Repair focus: hands and anatomy correction.
Keep the same identity, scene, wardrobe, and composition.
Fix hands, fingers, wrists, arms, shoulders, necks, and posture.
Hands should look natural, relaxed, and anatomically correct.
No extra fingers, fused fingers, missing fingers, broken wrists, or unnatural arm crossing.
```

### 5.4 地标错误或背景假

```text
Repair focus: landmark and environment realism.
Keep the same couple identity, outfit, pose, and shot type.
Correct the landmark so it is recognizable as {landmark} in {scene_name}.
Integrate the couple naturally into the environment with matching light, perspective, and scale.
Do not make the background look pasted, distorted, fantasy-like, or theme-park-like.
```

### 5.5 服装错误或质感廉价

```text
Repair focus: wardrobe accuracy and fabric quality.
Keep the same couple identity, location, pose, and composition.
Correct the outfit to match {outfit_name}.
Make the bride's wardrobe high-end, well-tailored, and realistic.
Make the groom's outfit coordinated, refined, and believable.
Improve fabric texture, seams, drape, veil, and accessory detail.
Avoid costume, stage dress, plastic shine, cheap lace, or messy tailoring.
```

### 5.6 构图不符合分镜

```text
Repair focus: composition alignment.
Keep the same identity, scene, outfit, and light.
Adjust the image to match the planned shot type: {shot_type}.
Required composition: {composition}.
Required interaction: {pose_interaction}.
Maintain {orientation} orientation.
Make the couple and landmark relationship natural, balanced, and visually clear.
```

### 5.7 光线色彩不自然

```text
Repair focus: light and color realism.
Keep the same couple identity, location, outfit, pose, and composition.
Correct the lighting to match: {light}.
Use natural cinematic light, realistic shadows, and elegant color grading.
Avoid oversaturated HDR, muddy colors, fake glow, harsh flash, and mismatched subject-background lighting.
```

### 5.8 清晰度不足或细节糊

```text
Repair focus: clarity and 4K detail.
Keep the same identity, scene, outfit, pose, composition, and light.
Improve facial clarity, fabric texture, hand detail, landmark detail, and background definition.
Maintain photorealistic 4K wedding photography quality.
Do not over-sharpen, add fake texture, or create waxy skin.
```

### 5.9 多余人物或画面杂乱

```text
Repair focus: subject isolation and clean frame.
Keep only the bride and groom as the main subjects.
Remove extra people, duplicated bodies, distracting tourists, random photographers, text, signs, and clutter.
Keep the background clean but still realistic for {scene_name}.
Preserve the planned landmark and travel atmosphere.
```
