## 1. Global Wardrobe Rules

- Bride wears no glasses in all wedding outfit anchors and final wedding images.
- Face must stay clear: no veil, hair, jewelry, fan, bouquet, or sleeve covering key facial features.
- Styling can change by scene, but face, age impression, body scale, and height relationship cannot change.
- Avoid cheap stagewear, cosplay, over-sexual styling, plastic fabric, heavy beauty filter, and influencer-template makeup.
- Groom styling should coordinate with the bride, but stay refined, simple, and believable.

## 2. Style Families

| Code | Style Family | Bride Styling | Groom Styling | Best Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| A | Main White Gown | White or ivory main gown, veil behind face, refined train | Black tuxedo or formal groom suit | Landmark cover, ceremony mood, wide and medium shots |
| B | Light Destination | Satin/chiffon light dress, easy movement, soft white or ivory | Beige, light gray, or soft neutral suit | Walking, travel, documentary, relaxed daylight shots |
| C | Evening Editorial | Black, burgundy, gold, or deep charcoal evening gown | Black tuxedo, velvet, or dark formal suit | Night scenes, architecture, low-angle editorial shots |
| D | French Couture | French lace, corset or square neckline, pearl/veil detail, elegant vintage mood | Black tuxedo, bow tie, refined boutonniere | Paris, Prague, Venice, cafe/street/architecture scenes |
| E | Modern Chinese | Red or ivory-red modern Chinese bridal set, refined embroidery, clean silhouette | Black or dark Tang-style detail suit, subtle red/gold accent | Oriental gardens, ceremonial frames, detail shots |
| F | Qipao | Modern bridal qipao, silk/satin texture, elegant high collar, refined slit | Dark suit or mandarin-collar formal jacket | Close-ups, evening streets, architectural lines, intimate portraits |

## 4. Prompt Additions

### French Couture

```text
French couture bridal styling, refined lace, elegant corset or square neckline, pearl detail, soft veil behind the face, vintage Paris editorial feeling, not theatrical, not costume-like.
```

### 新中式

```text
Modern Chinese bridal styling, refined red or ivory-red embroidered fabric, elegant ceremonial silhouette, high-end tailoring, culturally inspired but not cosplay, no heavy stage makeup, no face-covering fan.
```

### 旗袍

```text
Modern bridal qipao, refined silk or satin texture, elegant high collar, tasteful slit, graceful fitted silhouette, sophisticated and restrained, not over-sexualized, not cheap costume.
```
