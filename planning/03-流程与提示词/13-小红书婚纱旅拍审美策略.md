# 小红书婚纱旅拍审美策略

模式：`doc mode`  
用途：为 81 张世界旅拍婚纱照生成提供审美关键词、构图策略、动作策略、服装策略和提示词转译规则。  
边界：本文只整理风格策略，不替代 `planning/03-流程与提示词/04-婚纱生成工作流.md` 和 `planning/07-结构化清单/81张镜头清单.csv`。

## 1. 调研依据

沿用主工作流中已记录的来源，不新增联网调研：

- https://www.xiao-ad.com/al/1936.html
- https://www.xiao-ad.com/zx/1490.html
- https://m.hunliji.com/bai_ke/detail_70699
- https://www.fxbaogao.com/detail/4917620

可执行结论：

- 小红书婚纱旅拍内容更重视真实案例、真实情绪、旅拍体验、场景化种草和审美风格。
- 生成图应像真实新人在目的地完成的一组旅拍大片，而不是影楼模板照、AI 拼贴图或景区打卡照。
- 关键词必须同时约束风格、构图、动作、服装和负面项，避免只写抽象审美词导致画面漂移。

## 2. 总体审美方向

核心判断：小红书高赞婚纱旅拍不是“摆得越满越高级”，而是人物真实、关系自然、场景可信、服装高级、光影有情绪。

生成提示词主轴：

```text
Xiaohongshu-inspired luxury destination wedding photography, natural emotional storytelling, cinematic light, real couple interaction, recognizable landmark, elegant realistic color grading, high-end editorial wedding look, no studio-template posing
```

中文执行口径：

- 像真实旅拍：保留路面、风、发丝、衣料和轻微动态，不要过度精修。
- 像新人本人：人物脸、身高差、肤色、发型和相处方式优先级高于服装和背景。
- 像目的地照片：地标要可识别，但不能像贴图或假背景。
- 像高级婚纱大片：材质、剪裁和光线要贵气，动作要松弛。

## 3. 风格关键词转译

| 关键词 | 审美含义 | 可转成提示词 | 避免写法 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 电影感婚纱照 | 光影有叙事，画面像电影剧照 | cinematic destination wedding photograph, soft dramatic light, emotional storytelling, elegant color grading | movie poster, fantasy scene, excessive contrast |
| 真实旅拍 | 像真实新人旅行中拍摄 | real destination wedding travel photography, authentic couple interaction, natural environment details | tourist snapshot, stiff sightseeing pose |
| 情绪感大片 | 重眼神、亲密关系和瞬间 | intimate emotional portrait, soft eye contact, quiet romantic mood | exaggerated crying, melodramatic expression |
| 自然互动 | 动作来自两人关系，不像摆拍 | candid interaction, walking hand in hand, laughing softly, adjusting veil naturally | forced pose, looking at camera with fixed smile |
| 轻纪实 | 有抓拍感但画面仍精致 | documentary-style wedding photo, candid framing, natural motion blur kept subtle | messy street photo, low-quality candid |
| 胶片质感 | 色彩柔和，颗粒轻，肤色真实 | soft film-like texture, gentle grain, realistic skin tone, muted highlights | heavy vintage filter, yellow cast |
| 高级留白 | 不堆满元素，人物和地标有呼吸 | elegant negative space, clean composition, refined minimal background | empty background, tiny couple, blank sky only |
| 世界地标婚纱 | 地点明确但不压过人物 | recognizable landmark in the background, couple integrated naturally into the location | landmark pasted behind couple, distorted monument |
| 松弛感 | 身体语言自然，不端着 | relaxed body language, effortless elegance, casual intimate movement | slouching, careless posture |
| 氛围光影 | 利用清晨、傍晚、窗光、逆光 | golden hour glow, morning soft light, backlit veil, atmospheric shadows | oversaturated sunset, fake HDR sky |

## 4. 构图关键词转译

| 关键词 | 使用场景 | 可转成提示词 | 画面检查点 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 超广角地标封面 | 每个景区第 1 张 | ultra-wide establishing shot, full-body couple, landmark clearly recognizable, elegant negative space | 人物完整，地标不变形 |
| 中远景双人照 | 主纱大片 | medium-wide couple portrait, landmark behind them, balanced foreground and background | 人和地标关系自然 |
| 亲密半身 | 情绪、头纱、戒指 | intimate half-body portrait, veil and ring details, soft eye contact | 手部自然，脸不被遮挡 |
| 纵深街拍 | 城市、巷道、桥、石板路 | leading lines, street depth, walking toward camera, background softly layered | 线条引导到人物 |
| 前景遮挡 | 咖啡馆、花、窗、树影 | subtle foreground framing, partial soft foreground, candid composition | 遮挡不盖脸 |
| 低机位建筑透视 | 教堂、桥、塔、城堡 | low-angle architectural perspective, couple grounded in the scene | 建筑不压迫人物 |
| 背影故事感 | 桥、海边、雪地、远山 | back-view couple, looking toward the landmark, quiet story mood | 双人关系明确 |
| 服装细节近景 | 礼服、头纱、手部、花束 | close-up fabric texture, hands and veil details, luxury bridal craftsmanship | 不变成商品图 |
| 夜景或黄金时刻 | 收尾封面候选 | blue hour or golden hour cinematic portrait, ambient city lights, realistic skin exposure | 皮肤不过暗，灯光不假 |

组合模板：

```text
Composition: {shot type}, {camera distance}, {leading lines or negative space}, the couple is naturally integrated into {landmark}, foreground and background are realistic, no pasted backdrop
```

## 5. 动作关键词转译

| 动作关键词 | 情绪目标 | 可转成提示词 | 负面约束 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 牵手走向镜头 | 松弛、旅拍、抓拍 | walking hand in hand toward camera, natural stride, candid smile | no synchronized stiff walking |
| 女主回头看男主 | 亲密、真实互动 | bride turns back to look at groom, soft smile, natural shoulder movement | no exaggerated head turn |
| 男主轻扶头纱 | 仪式感、细节 | groom gently adjusts bride's veil, tender hand gesture, veil softly backlit | hands do not cover face |
| 两人额头轻靠 | 安静亲密 | foreheads gently touching, quiet intimate moment, relaxed closed distance | no forced kissing pose |
| 风吹裙摆 | 目的地感、动态 | wind catches the wedding dress train, fabric flowing naturally | no flying dress fantasy effect |
| 并肩看远方 | 故事感、场景感 | couple standing side by side, looking toward the horizon or landmark | no disconnected body language |
| 轻跑或快步 | 活力、抓拍 | light running movement, dress moving with steps, joyful but elegant | no sports-like running |
| 低声说笑 | 真实关系 | couple whispering and laughing softly, candid documentary feel | no exaggerated open-mouth laugh |
| 整理衣领或袖口 | 都市、纪实 | bride adjusts groom's collar, groom smiles naturally, editorial street style | no fashion catalog stiffness |

动作提示词原则：

- 每张只放 1 个主动作，最多加 1 个微表情。
- 动作必须能被镜头捕捉，不写抽象关系词代替动作。
- 手部动作必须补充 `natural hands, fingers anatomically correct, hands do not cover faces`。

## 6. 服装关键词转译

| 服装关键词 | 适合镜头 | 可转成提示词 | 禁止方向 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 高定拖尾白纱 | 封面、地标、仪式感 | couture white wedding gown, structured bodice, long elegant train, refined veil | cheap costume dress |
| 立体剪裁 | 主纱、都市、建筑感 | sculptural tailoring, clean structured silhouette, premium bridal craftsmanship | overdesigned stage costume |
| 缎面轻纱 | 街拍、咖啡馆、城市 | satin slip bridal dress, lightweight veil, relaxed travel wedding styling | shiny synthetic fabric |
| 短纱都市西装 | 纽约、街拍、动态 | short bridal dress, modern tailored suit, city editorial wedding look | party costume |
| 披肩轻纱 | 雪山、冷光、风 | soft bridal shawl, light tulle layers, warm elegant winter styling | bulky coat hiding silhouette |
| 沙滩轻薄纱 | 海岛、海风、赤脚 | airy beach wedding dress, barefoot seaside styling, translucent flowing fabric | swimwear-like bridal look |
| 丝绒礼服 | 夜景、复古、电影感 | velvet evening gown, deep rich tone, elegant old-world styling | theatrical costume |
| 地域融合礼服 | 京都、威尼斯、布拉格等 | location-inspired color details, subtle cultural reference, high-end modern bridal styling | literal cosplay, costume-like ethnic outfit |
| 精致头纱 | 半身、逆光、细节 | delicate veil texture, backlit veil edge, realistic transparent fabric | oversized plastic veil |

服装提示词原则：

- 先写材质和剪裁，再写颜色和配饰。
- 地域融合只做“色彩、纹理、局部元素”，不做戏服化复刻。
- 男主服装要和女主同等级：`tailored suit, premium fabric, clean fit`，避免廉价租赁感。

## 7. 禁用模板感

必须加入负面提示词的方向：

| 模板感问题 | 负面提示词 | 检查方式 |
|---|---|---|
| 影楼站桩 | no studio-template posing, no stiff symmetrical pose | 双人动作是否像同一模板复制 |
| 同时假笑看镜头 | no exaggerated smiles, no forced camera-facing grin | 表情是否自然 |
| 网红打卡照 | no tourist snapshot style, no landmark check-in pose | 地标是否只是背景板 |
| 过度磨皮 | no plastic skin, no heavy beauty filter | 肤质是否还有真实纹理 |
| 过饱和天空 | no oversaturated sky, no fake HDR | 天空和肤色是否可信 |
| 廉价礼服 | no cheap wedding dress, no synthetic shiny fabric | 婚纱是否像舞台服 |
| 假景区背景 | no pasted backdrop, no mismatched lighting | 人物光线是否匹配场景 |
| 夸张亲吻 | no forced kissing pose, no overly staged intimacy | 亲密是否自然 |
| 手部遮脸 | hands do not cover faces, natural hands | 五官是否完整可见 |
| AI 拼贴 | no collage look, no warped landmark, no floating couple | 人、地、光是否统一 |

通用负面提示词补充：

```text
no studio-template posing, no stiff symmetrical pose, no forced camera-facing grin, no tourist snapshot style, no pasted backdrop, no mismatched lighting, no plastic skin, no heavy beauty filter, no oversaturated sky, no fake HDR, no cheap wedding dress, no costume-like outfit, no distorted landmark, no floating couple
```

## 8. 提示词落地格式

每个关键词进入生成提示词时，按以下顺序落位：

1. 风格词进入 `Style`
2. 构图词进入 `Composition`
3. 动作词进入 `Pose and interaction`
4. 服装词进入 `Wardrobe`
5. 禁用模板感进入 `Negative prompt`

示例：

```text
Style:
- Xiaohongshu-inspired destination wedding photography
- cinematic destination wedding photograph
- natural emotional storytelling
- soft film-like texture
- elegant realistic color grading

Composition:
- vertical medium-wide street-depth composition
- leading lines from stone pavement guide the eye to the couple
- recognizable Paris street background, not pasted or distorted

Pose and interaction:
- the couple walks hand in hand toward camera
- the bride turns slightly toward the groom with a soft candid smile
- natural hands, hands do not cover faces

Wardrobe:
- Bride: satin slip bridal dress, lightweight veil, refined fabric texture
- Groom: tailored black suit, premium fabric, clean fit

Negative:
- no studio-template posing
- no forced camera-facing grin
- no tourist snapshot style
- no pasted backdrop
- no plastic skin
- no cheap wedding dress
```

## 10. 验收清单

生成前检查：

- 每张图至少有 1 个明确风格词、1 个构图词、1 个动作词、1 个服装材质词。
- 地标提示词包含“可识别但不贴图”的约束。
- 人物提示词优先保留身份、身高差、肤色、发型和自然表情。
- 负面提示词包含影楼模板、假笑、过度磨皮、假背景、廉价礼服。
- 动作没有重复堆叠，手部和面部没有互相遮挡。

生成后检查：

- 看起来像真实旅拍，不像影楼样片。
- 人物像本人，不像换脸或明星脸。
- 服装高级但不戏服化。
- 地标可信，光线统一。
- 画面适合小红书竖图浏览，主体清晰，留白克制。
